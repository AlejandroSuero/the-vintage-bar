Here’s what you’ll gain

Access to our growing community

The Vintage Bar has various platforms and social channels – most notably our Instagram with more than 600k followers – and as a partner on The Vintage Bar your items will be included in the content we produce and showcase on these platforms.

Get branding through our universe

We use branding in order to create an inspiring universe for our community and to make secondhand shopping cool and luxurious. As a partner on the platform you’ll be included in this as we’ll promote your boutique and pieces in the same way we do our own.

Connecting local supply with global demand

Partnering with a marketplace with a global community that also provides worldwide shipping makes it possible for your boutique to expand and reach new target groups that previously weren’t able to shop with you.
